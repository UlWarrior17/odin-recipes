Easy Caramel Apple Crisp Pie

This easy caramel apple crisp pie combines 2 of your favorite fall desserts—apple pie and apple crisp. Serve topped with ice cream or whipped cream and a drizzle of more caramel.


By Nicole McLaughlin

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 35 min

Stand Time: 20 mins

Total Time: 1 hr 5 mins

Servings: 8

Yield: 1 9-inch pie

Ingredients

  • 1 (9 inch) unbaked pie crust
  • 2 (20 ounce) cans apple pie filling
  • 1/3 cup thick caramel dip, (such as Marzetti® caramel dip), plus more for topping
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2/3 cup light brown sugar
  • 2/3 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup butter, cut into cubes
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Pour pie filling into crust.
  2. Heat caramel dip in a microwave on High until runny, about 20 seconds. Drizzle caramel over pie filling.
  3. Combine flour, brown sugar, oats, butter, cinnamon, and salt in a bowl and mix together with a fork until combined and
  4. crumbly. Scatter crumble mixture evenly over pie. Place pie on a rimmed baking sheet.

  5. Bake in the preheated oven until crust is golden brown and filling is bubbly around the edges, 35 to 45 minutes. Let
  6. stand for 20 minutes before serving.
